Web sites and applications are no longer just “pages,” they’re complex systems that must adapt to a variety of needs.

How do we create maintainable designs that offer consistency yet flexibility?  By embracing the power of modularity.

During this one-hour workshop, you’ll learn how to:

  • Design with modularity in mind.
  • Create a living, maintainable library of design patterns.
  • Sell the benefits of modular design to stakeholders.
